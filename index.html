What is Moist Esports?

Created on August 11th, 2021 on a whim by Youtuber Cr1TiKaL where he rallied votes for Kola to join Smash ULtimate Summit 3. We are an up and coming Esports organization taking the FGC by storm and will quickly expand our reach into other gaming categories.

Kola

Kolawole Aideyan

First player to join Moist Esports for our Smash Ultimate team and his favorite characters to play are Roy and Cloud.

He is currently ranked 46th in the world in Smash Ultimate.

Aaron

Aaron Wilhite

One of the best Diddy Kongs in the world of Smash Ultimate we have Aaron who is our second player to join Moist Esports.

Moky

Kurtis Pratt

Currently our only player that is on our Smash Melee team.

Recently came our of retirement to start playing Melee again at an extremely high level.

Goblin

Alexis Stennett

One of the best Smash Ultimate players from Florida being ranked 8th on the Central Florida Power Rankings.

He also mains Roy like Kola, but also has a strong secondary in Chrom.

Light

Paris Ramirez Garcia

Light is ranked in the top 10 best Smash Ultimate players in the world.

Light is also considered one of the best doubles player in the world playing along side his teammate Marss.

Hotashi

Julian Harris

Hotashi is our Guilty Gear Strive representative and considered one of the greatest players in it maining Nagoriyuki.

Boomie

Zack Bielamowicz

Currently ranked #2 in the Global Power Rankings and has won the brawlhalla world championship three times.

Known for playing Diana and holding the record for most top 8 placements in both 1v1 and 2v2 tournaments.
